Easy living



Details of photographic shots. Simplicity of shapes is what is being sought inside such shots. Overlaying of diverse materials. Immediateness, spontaneity, these are the keywords of a collection which narrates how quick things move in life and suggests to reflect on instantaneity and precariousness. The instant searches for a meaning and gives birth to the artwork.
