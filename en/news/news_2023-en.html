News 2023



AGCPUNTO.23 - RELOADED
pop-up show / exhibition | Archivio Negroni, Milano (Italy) | 2023, October, 20th

Within the context of the Milano Jewelry Week 2023 the Archivio Negroni in Milan hosted a pop-up show reminiscent of the full-format exhibition that AGC - Associazione gioiello contemporaneo had organized for its members in Guardiagrele (Chieti) in August 2023. Stefano Fronza took part in the show with its work Pearl Mountain.




RJW - AGC
exhibition | Alternatives Gallery, Roma (Italy) | 2023, October, 7th - 14th

Within the context of the Roma Jewelry Week 2023, Alternatives Gallery and AGC - Associazione gioiello contemporaneo collaborated in the selection of 16 jewelry artists, who were given the possibility to exhibit their works in the prestigious venue of the homonymous Gallery, located at the heart of Rome.
Stefano Fronza took part in the exhibition with 7 works created for the occasion and, along with the other selected artists, he got a review on the magazine Rewriters.




AGCPUNTO.23
exhibition / event | Palazzo dell’Artigianato, Guardiagrele (Italy) | 2023, August, 1st - 27th

The 53rd Mostra dell’artigianato artistico, which took place in August 2023 in Guardiagrele (Abruzzo), set the stage for a show exhibition organized by AGC - Associazione gioiello contemporaneo and curated by Roberta Pavone. As AGC member, Stefano Fronza participated in the exhibition with his brooch Pearl Mountain.

Pearl Mountain
brooch | 6,8x7,2x1,9 cm | Lambda print on aluminium and Forex, aluminium, PVC, pearl, titanium

Trentino Mese | magazine
publication - article | Trento (Italy) | May 2023

In May 2023 the magazine Trentino Mese published an article including an interview with Stefano Fronza. The article - entitled Stefano Fronza. Gioielli: abilità e maestria - sheds light on the peculiarities of this form of art and on the education as well as the working experience of the artist, whose multi-faceted aspect is therein revealed.




ARTE Y JOYA | CONTEMPORARY JEWELLERY YEARBOOK 2022/2023
selection, exhibition and publications | Barcellona (Spain) | 2022/2023

Organized by the Gruppo Duplex , the 2022/23 edition of Arte y Joya Yearbook counted on a list of 133 candidate artists from all over the world: out of this list, 34 finalists got selected by a jury of jewelry experts.
With his brooch Lightning Bolt Stefano Fronza was among the finalists, whose works have been published on a catalogue released for the occasion as well as on the Spanish magazine Contraste. During 2023 the 34 works that got selected as finalists have been exhibited at the National Museum of Decorative Arts in Madrid and, later during the year, at a prestigious event such as Melting Point in Valencia.




Purifying The Soul – The Soul is Responding
selection / exhibition | Lanng Space, Beijing (China) | 2023, February, 22th - 26th

Curated by Felicia Li from the Vonmo Studio Gallery in Beijing (China), the 2022/23 third edition of the contemporary jewelry project "Purifying The Soul" eventually came to conclusion with the exhibition held at the Lanng Space in Beijing on the 26th of February 2023. This project had been claiming the public's attention since the day it was first launched, i.e. since the 4th of October 2021. A total of 220 artists from 38 countries had made sure to deliver their “soul objects” within the 10th of June 2022. After a careful evaluation by an international jury, Stefano Fronza's work Risorsa ended up being selected for the final exhibition.

Risorsa
spilla | 8,6x9x1,4 cm | stampa su Dibond, alluminio, titanio
