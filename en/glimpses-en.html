Glimpses through the window



Out of the window there's a reality that is alienated by the glass pane in between. The overlaying of diverse materials goes in search of the shapes hidden in photographs. The traits of a leaf made of pure gold provide contrast and light to the finished objects while recalling and paying tribute to the ancient goldsmith tradition.
