Between Music and Rebirth

Between Music and Rebirth



Cardboard models originating from paper drawings. Fragments of printed photographs provide more volume to the finished object. The juxtaposition, overlaying, distancing of various elements act side by side in the artwork just like musicians who play together, creating an harmonious balance and that very music gives form to a shape.


Jewelry photos by Nadia Baldo
